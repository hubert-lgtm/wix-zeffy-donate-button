What we do

Maple Ridge takes in dogs and cats most shelters can't keep — seniors, medical cases, owner surrenders with nowhere else to go. We don't have a building. Every animal lives in a foster home until adoption, where they get vet care, behavior rehab, and the time it takes to settle.
