What we do

Maple Ridge takes in dogs and cats most shelters can't keep — seniors, medical cases, owner-surrenders with nowhere else to go.

Every animal lives in a foster home until adoption. Vet care, behavior rehab, and the time it takes to settle — that's the model. The money goes straight to the animals.

How Maple Ridge works

From overcrowded shelter to your couch.

Four steps, every animal. The whole thing takes anywhere from two weeks to six months.

1

Pull

A county shelter calls. We say yes when we have space. We say sorry when we don't, and we hate that part.

~40 partner shelters
2

Foster

A foster meets us at the parking lot. We hand over food, a crate, and a vet contact. The animal goes home with someone the same day.

89 active foster homes
3

Heal

Vet visits, spay/neuter, vaccines, dental, heartworm tx if needed. We don't put up an animal until they're medically ready.

$78,400 in vet care, 2025
4

Adopt

Application, home check, meet-and-greet. Then a $250 adoption fee, a goodbye photo at the foster's front door, and a forever-home update group chat.

312 adoptions, 2025

Where your money goes.

We don't take government funding. Every dollar comes from people like you, and it goes straight to the animals — vet bills, food, and the few things our fosters can't supply themselves.

  • Vet intake exam $85
  • Spay or neuter $220
  • Heartworm treatment ~$600
  • Monthly food + litter, one foster $45
  • Parvo treatment, full case ~$1,200
