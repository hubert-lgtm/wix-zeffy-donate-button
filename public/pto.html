Our mission

Lincoln PTO is the parent-run engine behind the extras that make school feel special — field trips, library books, classroom supplies, and the teacher-appreciation week our staff actually look forward to.

We don't run the school. We just make sure no teacher pays for markers out of pocket, and no kid gets left off the bus because their family couldn't cover a fee.

A note from the PTO

"My kid came home last fall asking why some kids didn't get to go on the field trip."

That conversation is why this PTO exists. We're not here to put on fancy galas or shake down working families — we're here to quietly close the gaps no one talks about. A coat. A trip. A book the library couldn't afford. The little things that decide whether school feels like a place a kid wants to be.

If you're new to Lincoln, welcome. If you're a returning family, thank you. Either way, we'd love your $25.

— Maya
