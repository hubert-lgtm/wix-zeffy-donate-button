Our mission

Lincoln PTO is the parent-run engine behind the extras that make school feel special — field trips, library books, classroom supplies, and the teacher appreciation week our staff actually look forward to. We don't run the school. We just make sure no teacher pays for markers out of pocket and no kid gets left off the bus because their family couldn't cover a fee.
